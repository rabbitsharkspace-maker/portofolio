Workflow Automation

Automated Community Ticketing System

A residential community was managing 100+ daily service requests manually — slow, error-prone, and exhausting for staff. We built an end-to-end AI system: residents submit requests via WeCom, messages are decrypted and passed to Gemini AI, which auto-classifies by category, priority, and assigns to the right team — all logged instantly to Google Sheets with a full audit trail.

Result: 100+ daily requests handled automatically. Response time from hours to seconds. Zero manual entry. Zero missed tickets.
